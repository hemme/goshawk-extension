SGF from Image

Semiautomatic converter from diagram images of the game Go (Weiqi, Baduk) to SGF format


Format:

1. Drag from 1-1 (corner) 1. Click 1-1 (corner)
2. Release at the limit grid
3. Click 2-2
2. Click 2-2
3. Click the limit grid
Done! (copied to clipboard)
Click wrong detection on board, if any.
Output:
Align H:
Align V:
To play:

Use cursor keys after each click for fine tuning (Shift or Ctrl + cursor keys for other points). If auto-adjustment fails in usage, try another style in usage2.
Shift+drag to magnify a region.
To correct tilt/perspective images, shift+click the top right corner and other three corners of the board counterclockwise before Step 1.
